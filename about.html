What People Think We Do vs. What We Actually Do

This is a great social media trend, especially on TikTok where our audience can gain valuable insight into what it's actually like in our music world. Here's a good example of this trend in action on TikTok videos. We set it up with an expectation of what people think it's like when we're making, then make a transition and show off the reality!

Our Writing/Production Process

Often, the journey is more interesting than the destination. All those hours, blood and sweat, and tears that we pour into our new song or latest release. We need to document this process and split it up for fans to see. Think about our favourite artists. Wouldn't we love to see behind-the-scenes content of how they actually compose their music?

Our Biggest Achievement

It's okay to talk about the things we have achieved on our social media channels. The music industry is tough and if we've achieved something music-related that is truly incredible, we should share it with people! Think about creative ways to share our achievements but remember, short form video is key when putting together micro-content as a musician.
